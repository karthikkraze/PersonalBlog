My Interests

Beyond my academic pursuits, I have always had a creative side that I love to express through art. Pencil art and digital art are two of my passions, where I can let my imagination run wild and create visually captivating pieces. The process of bringing ideas to life on paper or digitally brings me immense joy and satisfaction.

Stepping into the realm of digital art opened up a whole new universe of creative possibilities for me. The marriage of technology and artistic expression captivated my imagination, leading me on a journey of exploration and self-discovery. In this blog, I aim to delve into the enchanting world of digital art, sharing my personal experiences, insights, and the allure that drew me towards this captivating medium.

Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has gained significant popularity worldwide. It encompasses a wide range of genres, including fantasy, romance, action, sci-fi, and more. What sets anime apart is its distinct art style, vibrant characters, engaging storylines, and cultural nuances....

Manga is a term used to refer to Japanese comics and graphic novels. It is a popular form of storytelling in Japan and has gained significant recognition and readership worldwide. Manga covers a wide range of genres, including action, romance, fantasy, science fiction, horror, comedy, and more.

Cricket plays a vital role in the lives of many Indian children and holds a special place in the hearts of the nation. It does for me too as it inspires me every second and teaches me what to do under pressure situations.
