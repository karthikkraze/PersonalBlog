Anime Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has gained significant popularity worldwide. It encompasses a wide range of genres, including fantasy, romance, action, sci-fi, and more. What sets anime apart is its distinct art style, vibrant characters, engaging storylines, and cultural nuances.
Manga Manga is a term used to refer to Japanese comics and graphic novels. It is a popular form of storytelling in Japan and has gained significant recognition and readership worldwide. Manga covers a wide range of genres, including action, romance, fantasy, science fiction, horror, comedy, and more.
